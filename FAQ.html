11   What is ERC-20 Token?

An ERC20 token is a blockchain-based asset with similar functionality to Bitcoin.

It can hold value and be sent and received.

The ERC20 tokens are stored and sent using Ethereum addresses and transactions, and use gas to cover transaction fees.
